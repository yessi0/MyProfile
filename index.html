For our first recipe we will have a chocoflan
- For your ingredients you will need:
  • media crema (354g)
  • condensed milk(375g)
  • evaporated Milk(375g)
  • 1 TBS vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • oil (to grease the mold)
  • Hot water (for el ba√±o maria)
  • Aluminum Foil
  • chocoalte cake mix and its ingredients
First you will start by preheating your oven to 350 degrees farenheit. While the oven is heating you can add your lightly greased bundt pan. Begin carmelizing sugar but putting it on a hot pan and stirring continuously. Make sure not to burn the sugar or it will taste too sour. Take your hot bundt pan out of the oven and pour the carmelized sugar into it and spread around the bottom by swirling it around. Leave this to cool on the side.
Now put 4 eggs, media crema, condensed milk, evaporated milk,and Vanilla extract to your blender and pour it over the carmelized sugar. Leave this in the oven for 30 minutes.These 30 are so that those ingrediants can begin seperation from the chocolate cake youre about to pour in.
Now you can mix all your chocoalte cake ingredients into a bowl and mix well. Take your bundt pan out of the oven so that you can pour the cake into it. Now just leave for 2 hours in the oven. Allow for this to cool, and stick a knife close to the edges just to make sure it isnt stuck and flip the pan!
